BLACKPINK

BLACKPINK (블 랙 핑 크) is YG Entertainment was launched on August 8, 2016 Korean women's singing group, by JISOO, JENNIE, ROSE, LISA of four members. The group name "Blackpink" adds a slightly negative meaning to the pink that looks good, aiming to convey the message that "don't just look at the pretty part" and "see not the whole thing". On August 8, 2016, she released her first single album "Square One" and made her debut. Single album in Indonesia, Malaysia and other 14 countries in the iTunes album chart won the number one, "Whistle" in eight music website weekly chart number one, is the first debut song set the record . On the 21st, Whistle won the title of "Popular Ballad", setting the record of the fastest Korean girl group to win a title. On June 22, 2017, the single "As If It's Your Last" was released. On June 5, 2018, the mini-album Square Up was released; On September 23, the single "So Hot" was released. On October 25, the single "Kiss and Make Up" with Dua Ripa was released. On April 5, 2019, the mini-album Kill This Love was released. On August 30, 2020, she won the "Summer Song Award" of the 37th MTV Video Music Awards with "How You Like That" . On October 2, she released her first regular ALBUM, The Album.
